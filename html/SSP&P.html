Saints Peter and Paul Church

This church was active from 1860 up until 1992, when it was abandoned. The East Liberty parish was founded so its members didn't have to travel to the Allegheny River Valley. The building was sold to Everlasting Covenant Church in 1997 for restoration, but the project was abandoned in 2002. The site is now a landmark for those looking to explore abandoned buildings. It was featured in the 1999 film 'Dogma'. While trying to keep warm with fire, people living inside the abandoned building inadvertently set fire to the floor in 2012.
