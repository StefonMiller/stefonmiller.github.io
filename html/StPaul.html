Saint Paul Cathedral

Another gothic revival structure, much like the Cathedral of Learning, Saint Paul Cathedral was constructed in 1906 and features beautiful interior and exterior architecture. It was designed by Chicago architects Egan and Prindeville. For anyone interested in gothic architecture, Saint Paul is a must-see. In addition to the architecture, the cathedral holds a breathtaking organ displayed inside. The exterior itself was constructed of limestone, and features 2 spires.
