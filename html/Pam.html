Pamela's Diner

Established in 1979, this Pittsburgh-local chain of diners serves various breakfast food as its specialty. Most known for their pancakes and crepes. The diner has seen national recognition, being mentioned by the Wall Street Journal and even seeing patronage from former president Barrack Obama. The chain has locations in Oakland, Shadyside, Mt. Lebanon, Squirrel Hill, the Strip District, and Millvale.
