Homewood Cemetery

Homewood cemetery, located next to Frick Park, was constructed in 1878. Much like the Allegheny Cemetery, is the home of countless beautiful sculptures and masonry. It holds the graves of many famous people, such as Henry C. Frick, Jock Sutherland, and Chuck Cooper. The stonework combined with its park setting make for an incredibly scenic experience.
